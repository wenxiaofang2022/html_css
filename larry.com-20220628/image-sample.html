A PACKAGING SYSTEM AS A FOCAL POINT

The packaging for Tong Heng is a focal point for brand engagement and differentiation. In discussing what 'heritage' meant to Tong Heng, when so many brands claimed some form of 'heritage', the answer fell back on the name and the tradition: bringing joy to people in every pastry Tong Heng makes.

The colours are both aesthetic and functional – yellow for everyday orders; red for anniversaries, baby showers, betrothals and Chinese New Year; turquoise for Mid-Autumn Festival. The bright colours add cheer to the store and help staff to quickly discern and pack respective purchases. Inside, we designed a modular insert that is adaptable to holding differently shaped pastries in a secure manner.

Items include carrier bags, individual sleeves, gusset bags and kaya bottle labels.
